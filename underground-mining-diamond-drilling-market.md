<p><strong>The Underground Mining Diamond Drilling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary Drilling</li><li>Wireline Drilling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The underground mining diamond drilling market comprises primarily two types: rotary drilling and wireline drilling. </p><p>Rotary drilling utilizes a rotating drill bit to penetrate rock, allowing for the collection of core samples, while continuously circulating drilling fluid to cool the bit and remove debris. </p><p>Wireline drilling, on the other hand, involves a specialized system that allows for the rapid retrieval of drill cores without pulling the entire drill string from the hole, thus enhancing efficiency and minimizing downtime. Both methods are essential for exploration and resource evaluation.</p></p>
