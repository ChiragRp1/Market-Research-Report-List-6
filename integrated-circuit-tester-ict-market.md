<p><p>Integrated Circuit Testers (ICT) are essential for assessing the functionality and reliability of various electronic components. The market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Logic Circuit Testers** evaluate digital circuits by testing logic states and signal integrity, ensuring accurate operations of digital systems. </p><p>2. **Memory Circuit Testers** specialize in assessing memory components, focusing on read/write functionalities and data retention. </p><p>3. **Mixed-signal Circuit Testers** handle both analog and digital signals, critical for devices that incorporate diverse signal types, enabling comprehensive testing for complex integrated circuits.</p></p>
