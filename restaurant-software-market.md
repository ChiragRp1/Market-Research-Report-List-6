<p><p>Traditional on-premise restaurant software market refers to software that is installed and run on the restaurant's own servers and infrastructure. This type of software requires a significant upfront investment in hardware and IT resources. On the other hand, the cloud (SaaS Delivery Model) market offers software that is hosted and accessed over the internet. This allows for greater flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness since businesses pay a subscription fee for access to the software without the need for extensive hardware and maintenance.</p></p>
