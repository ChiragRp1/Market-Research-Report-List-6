<p><ul><li>EUV</li><li>Non EUV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reticle POD (Pellicle Optical Demagnification) is used in lithography equipment to protect photomasks from particles and moisture during the manufacturing process. There are two main types of Reticle POD markets: EUV and Non-EUV. EUV Reticle PODs are specifically designed for extreme ultraviolet lithography, which is used in advanced semiconductor manufacturing processes. Non-EUV Reticle PODs are used in traditional optical lithography processes. Both types of Reticle PODs play a critical role in ensuring the accuracy and quality of semiconductor devices produced in the market.</p></p>
