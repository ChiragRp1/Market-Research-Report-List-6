<p><strong>The Wafer Pre Aligner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vacuum Aligner</li><li>Atmosphere Aligner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wafer pre-alignment market is categorized into two main types: vacuum aligners and atmosphere aligners. Vacuum aligners utilize suction to secure a wafer in place, ensuring precise positioning by minimizing mechanical vibrations and environmental interference. They are often employed in high-precision applications. On the other hand, atmosphere aligners operate in ambient conditions, providing simpler alignment mechanisms and being suitable for less stringent applications. These aligners are essential for enhancing yield and efficiency in semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
