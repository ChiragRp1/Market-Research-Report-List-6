<p><strong>The Polycarbonate Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Phosgene Type</li><li>Non-Phosgene Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polycarbonate resin market is primarily divided into two types: phosgene and non-phosgene. Phosgene-type polycarbonate is produced using phosgene as a key intermediate in the polymerization process, offering high optical clarity and mechanical strength. In contrast, non-phosgene polycarbonate is synthesized using alternative methods, eliminating the use of phosgene, which may be viewed as a safer option. This type often emphasizes eco-friendliness while maintaining desirable properties, catering to industries looking for sustainable materials without compromising performance.</p></p>
