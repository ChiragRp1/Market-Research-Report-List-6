Ethyl acrylate polymers are valued for their flexibility and are commonly used in pressure-sensitive adhesives. Butyl acrylate polymers provide high adhesion and water resistance, suitable for sealants and coatings. Other acrylate polymers include various functionalized forms tailored for specialized applications, enhancing versatility in the market.</p></p>
