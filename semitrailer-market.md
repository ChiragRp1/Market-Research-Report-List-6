<p><p>The semitrailer market consists of various types, each designed for specific cargo needs. Lowboy semitrailers have a lowered deck, suitable for carrying oversized equipment. Dry van semitrailers offer enclosed spaces for general cargo, ensuring protection from the elements. Refrigerated semitrailers maintain temperature control for perishable goods. Other types include specialty trailers, catering to niche markets like car carriers or tankers.</p></p>
