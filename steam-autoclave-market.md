<p><strong>The Steam Autoclave Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre-vacuum Autoclave</li><li>Steam Flush Autoclave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steam autoclave market is primarily segmented into pre-vacuum autoclaves and steam flush autoclaves. Pre-vacuum autoclaves utilize a vacuum system to remove air before steam penetration, enhancing sterilization efficacy for porous materials. In contrast, steam flush autoclaves operate by simultaneously injecting steam to displace air, making them suitable for less demanding sterilization needs. Both types are essential in healthcare and laboratory settings, ensuring effective sterilization of instruments and materials to prevent infections and maintain standards.</p></p>
