<p><p>Torque motor rotary tables are critical in precision positioning applications, categorized mainly into two types: external rotation structure and internal rotation structure. External rotation structures feature torque motors mounted outside the rotating platform, providing high torque and efficiency, ideal for heavy loads. In contrast, internal rotation structures integrate torque motors within the table itself, offering compact designs with reduced external footprint, suitable for space-limited environments. Both types enhance operational accuracy and speed, catering to diverse industrial requirements.</p></p>
