<p><p>The Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) pads market is divided into two main types: hard CMP pads and soft CMP pads. Hard CMP pads are typically used for polishing harder materials, providing a more aggressive material removal rate, which is ideal for deep etching and planarization. Soft CMP pads, on the other hand, offer a gentler touch, suitable for delicate surfaces and fine polishing, allowing for smoother finishes. Both types are essential in semiconductor manufacturing for achieving precision and high-quality surface finishes.</p></p>
