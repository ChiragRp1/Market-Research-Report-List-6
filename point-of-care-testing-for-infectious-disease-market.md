<p><p>Point of Care Testing (POCT) for infectious diseases encompasses two main market types: immunological tests and pathogen nucleic acid tests. Immunological tests detect specific antibodies or antigens related to pathogens, facilitating rapid diagnosis and management of infections like influenza or HIV. In contrast, pathogen nucleic acid tests identify the genetic material of pathogens, providing precise results for infections such as COVID-19 or tuberculosis. Both testing methods enhance clinical decision-making by enabling timely treatment and containment measures at the patient's location.</p></p>
