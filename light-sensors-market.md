<p><ul><li>Contact Light Sensor</li><li>Non-Contact Light Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The light sensor market is divided into two main types: contact and non-contact light sensors. Contact light sensors directly interact with the light source, often used in applications like automatic streetlights or industrial environments where light levels need to be monitored closely. Non-contact light sensors, on the other hand, detect light without physical interaction, making them suitable for smart devices and environmental monitoring. Both types play crucial roles in automation, energy efficiency, and enhancing user experiences across various industries.</p></p>
