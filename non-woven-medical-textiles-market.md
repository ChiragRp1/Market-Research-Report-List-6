<p><p>Non woven medical textiles are fabrics that are produced by bonding or interlocking fibers using mechanical, thermal, or chemical processes, without the need for weaving or knitting. These textiles are widely used in the healthcare industry for various applications such as surgical gowns, drapes, bandages, wound dressings, and face masks.</p><p>The Non woven Medical Textiles Market is witnessing significant growth due to the rising demand for disposable medical products, increasing awareness about infection control, and the growing healthcare sector globally. The market is also being driven by advancements in technology, which have led to the development of innovative non woven materials with enhanced properties such as breathability, absorbency, and barrier protection.</p><p>Furthermore, the COVID-19 pandemic has further accelerated the demand for non woven medical textiles, as there has been a surge in the production of personal protective equipment (PPE) to combat the spread of the virus.
