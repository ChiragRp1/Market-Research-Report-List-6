<p><p>Montan wax is categorized into two primary market types: crude montan wax and refined montan wax. Refined montan wax undergoes further processing to remove impurities, resulting in a purer product with enhanced properties. This refined variant is favored in industries such as cosmetics, plastics, and coatings due to its superior performance and versatility, while crude montan wax is generally utilized for less demanding applications.</p></p>
