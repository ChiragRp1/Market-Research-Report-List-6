<p><p>The Silicon Carbide discrete devices market encompasses three main types: SiC MOSFETs, SiC diodes, and SiC modules. SiC MOSFETs are used for high-efficiency power switching applications due to their excellent thermal conductivity and fast switching speeds. SiC diodes, known for their low forward voltage drop and high-temperature operation, improve efficiency in rectification processes. SiC modules combine multiple SiC components into a single unit, optimizing space and enhancing performance in power electronics, particularly in renewable energy and electric vehicles.</p></p>
