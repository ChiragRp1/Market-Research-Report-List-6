<p><p>The photo mask market comprises two primary types: quartz masks and soda-lime glass masks. Quartz masks are made from high-purity quartz and are used in advanced lithography processes due to their superior transparency and thermal stability, making them ideal for complex semiconductor applications. In contrast, soda-lime glass masks are more economical and are suitable for less demanding applications. Each type serves specific industry needs, balancing cost and performance in photolithography for semiconductor manufacturing and other precision applications.</p></p>
