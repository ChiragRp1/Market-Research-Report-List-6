<p><p>The magnesium sulfate market comprises two primary types: anhydrous magnesium sulfate and hydrous magnesium sulfate. Anhydrous magnesium sulfate, a dry, crystalline form, is primarily used in agriculture as a fertilizer and in industrial applications such as textiles and chemicals. Hydrous magnesium sulfate, often found in nature as Epsom salt, contains water and is widely utilized in pharmaceuticals, personal care products, and food processing. Each type serves distinct industries, driving demand based on specific application needs.</p></p>
