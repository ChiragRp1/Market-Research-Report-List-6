<p><p>The Optical Surgical Navigation System market can be categorized into two main types: Touch Type and Touchless Type. Touch Type systems require direct interaction, where surgeons physically manipulate a touchscreen or interface to navigate and access surgical data during procedures. Conversely, Touchless Type systems utilize advanced technologies such as hand gestures, voice commands, or eye-tracking, allowing surgeons to interact without physical contact. Both types enhance precision and efficiency in surgical operations, improving overall patient outcomes and streamlining the surgical workflow.</p></p>
