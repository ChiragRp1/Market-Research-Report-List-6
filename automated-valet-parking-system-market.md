<p><p>Automated Valet Parking System market consists of two main types: Millimeter Wave Radar and Surround View. Millimeter Wave Radar technology uses high-frequency radio waves to detect objects around the vehicle, providing accurate positioning and guidance for automated parking. On the other hand, Surround View technology utilizes multiple cameras positioned around the vehicle to create a 360-degree view, helping drivers navigate tight parking spaces with ease. Both technologies aim to enhance the efficiency and convenience of valet parking systems.</p></p>
