<p><ul><li>Thrombectomy Devices</li><li>Atherectomy Devices</li><li>CTO Devices</li><li>EPD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plaque modification devices are used for the removal of plaque build-up in blood vessels. Thrombectomy devices are designed to remove blood clots, while atherectomy devices are used to cut or shave off plaque from artery walls. Chronic total occlusion (CTO) devices help clear completely blocked arteries. Embolic protection devices (EPDs) are used during procedures to prevent debris or blood clots from traveling to other parts of the body. These devices play a crucial role in minimizing the risk of complications and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
