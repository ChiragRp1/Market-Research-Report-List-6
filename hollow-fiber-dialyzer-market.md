<p><ul><li>Low Flux Membrane</li><li>High Flux Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hollow Fiber Dialyzer Market includes two main types of membranes: Low Flux Membrane and High Flux Membrane. Low Flux Membrane has smaller pore sizes which are suitable for removing small molecules and waste products from the blood during dialysis. On the other hand, High Flux Membrane has larger pore sizes that allow for the removal of larger molecules, making it more efficient in clearing toxins. Both types of membranes play a crucial role in providing effective dialysis treatment for patients with kidney failure.</p></p>
