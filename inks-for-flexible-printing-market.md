<p><ul><li>Water-Based Inks</li><li>Solvent-Based Inks</li><li>UV-Cured Inks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flexible printing inks market comprises three primary types: water-based inks, solvent-based inks, and UV-cured inks. Water-based inks are eco-friendly and suitable for a variety of substrates, offering good adhesion and vibrant colors. Solvent-based inks, known for their durability and resistance, perform well on non-porous surfaces but may emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs). UV-cured inks use ultraviolet light to instantly cure, providing excellent gloss and chemical resistance, making them ideal for high-quality packaging and labels.</p></p>
