<p><strong>The Confocal Chromatic Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>Confocal chromatic displacement sensors are used to measure distances, displacements, and positions with high precision in various industrial applications. These sensors utilize the chromatic aberration effect to determine the distance between the sensor and the target. On the other hand, confocal chromatic thickness sensors are designed to measure the thickness of transparent materials with accuracy and reliability. Both types of sensors are essential in industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and electronics for quality control and monitoring processes.</p></p>
