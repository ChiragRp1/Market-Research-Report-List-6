<p><p>Manual pipettes are essential laboratory tools used for precise liquid handling. The market is primarily divided into single-channel and multichannel pipettes. Single-channel pipettes allow for the transfer of one liquid sample at a time, making them ideal for tasks requiring accuracy and minimal sample volume. Multichannel pipettes can dispense multiple samples simultaneously, improving efficiency in high-throughput applications, such as in drug discovery or molecular biology. Both types cater to diverse laboratory needs, enhancing productivity and precision in various research fields.</p></p>
