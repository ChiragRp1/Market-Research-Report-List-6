<p><p>The liposuction device market comprises various types, primarily including laser-assisted and ultrasound-assisted devices. In contrast, ultrasound-assisted liposuction devices utilize ultrasonic waves to emulsify fat, facilitating its removal while minimizing damage to surrounding tissues.
