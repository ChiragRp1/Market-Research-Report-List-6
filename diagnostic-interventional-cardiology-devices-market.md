<p><p>The Diagnostic Interventional Cardiology Devices market encompasses various technologies designed for minimally invasive cardiovascular procedures. Transcatheter Angiography Devices facilitate the visualization of blood vessels, aiding in diagnosis and treatment of blockages. Intravascular Ultrasound (IVUS) Devices provide real-time imaging within blood vessels, enhancing the understanding of atherosclerosis and guiding interventions. Other devices in this market may include pressure wire systems or optical coherence tomography systems, all aimed at improving diagnostic accuracy and patient outcomes in interventional cardiology procedures.</p></p>
