<p><p>Satellite-Based Automatic Identification Systems (S-AIS) enhance maritime safety by using satellites to track vessels' movements globally. The market is divided into Class A and Class B transponders. Class A transponders are mandatory for larger vessels, offering extensive communication and safety features, including location, course, and speed data. Class B transponders, typically used by smaller vessels and recreational crafts, provide essential information but with limited range and functionalities. Both transponder types contribute significantly to maritime situational awareness and ocean governance.</p></p>
