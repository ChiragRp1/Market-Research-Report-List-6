<p><p>The microdisplay market consists of various technologies, including LCD monitors, Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCoS), and Organic Light Emitting Diodes (OLED). LCD monitors utilize liquid crystal technology to produce images, offering good color reproduction and brightness. LCoS is a reflective display technology providing high resolution and compact size, ideal for projectors and head-mounted displays. OLED displays use organic compounds to emit light directly, resulting in vibrant colors and deeper blacks, making them suitable for premium devices and applications requiring high visual fidelity.</p></p>
