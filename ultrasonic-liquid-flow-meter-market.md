<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Liquid Flow Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inline Type</li><li>Clamp-On Type</li><li>Insertion Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasonic liquid flow meter market comprises three key types: inline, clamp-on, and insertion types. Inline flow meters are installed directly in the piping system, providing accurate measurements for various fluids. Clamp-on flow meters attach externally to the pipe, using ultrasonic signals to measure flow without interrupting the system, making them ideal for retrofitting. Insertion flow meters penetrate the pipe wall to gauge flow from within, offering a balance between accuracy and ease of installation in specific applications.</p></p>
