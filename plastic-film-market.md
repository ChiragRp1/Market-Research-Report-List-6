<p><p>The plastic film market includes several key types: Polyethylene (PE), Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP), and Biaxially Oriented Polyethylene Terephthalate (BOPET). PE films are widely used for packaging due to their moisture resistance and flexibility. BOPP films are known for their clarity and strength, making them ideal for food packaging and labels. BOPET films offer excellent tensile strength and thermal stability, often utilized in electronics and high-barrier packaging. Together, these materials cater to diverse applications across various industries.</p></p>
