<p><strong>The Unit Sorters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cross-belt Sorters</li><li>Tilt tray Sorters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Unit sorters come in various types, primarily including cross-belt sorters and tilt tray sorters, each designed for specific applications in logistics and distribution. Cross-belt sorters utilize a conveyor with individual belts to divert items at high speeds, making them ideal for handling a large volume of small packages. Tilt tray sorters, on the other hand, feature trays that tilt to release items at predetermined locations, effectively managing larger items. Both sorter types enhance efficiency and accuracy in order fulfillment processes.</p></p>
