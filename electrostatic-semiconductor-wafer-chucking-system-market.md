<p><strong>The Electrostatic Semiconductor Wafer Chucking System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type Electrostatic Chucks</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Electrostatic Chucks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electrostatic Semiconductor Wafer Chucking System market is primarily categorized into two types: Coulomb Type Electrostatic Chucks and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Electrostatic Chucks. Coulomb Type chucks utilize electrostatic forces to hold wafers through charges generated on their surfaces, offering stability during processing. In contrast, JR Type chucks rely on the mechanism of electric field-induced attraction between the chuck and the wafer, allowing for enhanced gripping without physical contact, thus minimizing contamination and damage to delicate semiconductor materials.</p></p>
