<p><ul><li>Impregnated Paper Insulated Cable</li><li>Oil-filled Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flexible Submarine Cable market comprises various types, primarily Impregnated Paper Insulated Cable and Oil-filled Cable. Impregnated Paper Insulated Cable utilizes layers of paper impregnated with insulating oil for enhanced performance and reliability, suitable for environments with varying temperatures. On the other hand, Oil-filled Cable employs a liquid insulation medium, offering superior thermal conductivity and electrical performance, making it ideal for deep-water applications. Both types are essential for efficient undersea communication and power transmission systems.</p></p>
