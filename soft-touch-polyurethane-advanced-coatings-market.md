Water-based coatings utilize water as a primary solvent, offering eco-friendliness, low VOC emissions, and ease of application, making them suitable for various surfaces. In contrast, solvent-based coatings use organic solvents, providing excellent durability and resistance to harsh conditions, often preferred in commercial applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, furniture, and consumer goods, ensuring versatile performance and aesthetics.</p></p>
