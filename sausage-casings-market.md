<p><ul><li>Natural Sausage Casings</li><li>Artificial Sausage Casings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sausage casings market is primarily divided into two types: natural and artificial casings. Natural sausage casings are derived from animal intestines, offering a traditional texture and flavor. They are biodegradable and preferred for premium sausages. On the other hand, artificial sausage casings are made from synthetic materials such as collagen, cellulose, or plastic, providing uniformity, durability, and ease of handling. These casings cater to mass production, ensuring consistent quality and shelf-life for various sausage types.</p></p>
