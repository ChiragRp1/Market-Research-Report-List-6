<p><p>The folding box sealers market is divided into two main types: uniform case sealers and random case sealers. Uniform case sealers are designed to handle boxes of the same size and shape, providing consistent sealing for streamlined production processes. In contrast, random case sealers can accommodate various box sizes, making them versatile for operations with diverse packaging needs. Both types play critical roles in enhancing efficiency and automation in packaging industries, catering to different operational requirements.</p></p>
