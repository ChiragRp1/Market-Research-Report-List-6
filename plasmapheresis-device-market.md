<p><p>A plasmapheresis device is a medical apparatus used for the separation and removal of plasma from blood, which is crucial in treating various autoimmune diseases, neurological disorders, and other conditions. This therapeutic process is instrumental in managing conditions like Guillain-Barré syndrome, myasthenia gravis, and certain kidney disorders.</p><p>The plasmapheresis device market is poised for significant growth, expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.70% during the forecast period. <p><ul><li>Source Plasma Collection</li><li>Therapeutic Plasmapheresis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plasmapheresis device market is categorized into two main types: source plasma collection and therapeutic plasmapheresis. Source plasma collection focuses on obtaining plasma for use in manufacturing immunoglobulins and clotting factors, while therapeutic plasmapheresis involves the removal of harmful substances from a patient's plasma to treat various medical conditions, such as autoimmune disorders. Both market types are essential for improving patient outcomes and enhancing the availability of plasma-derived therapies, driving growth in the medical device sector.</p></p>
