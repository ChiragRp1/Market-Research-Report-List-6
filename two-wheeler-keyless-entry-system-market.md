<p><p>The Two-Wheeler Keyless Entry System market includes two primary types: Passive Keyless Entry System (PKES) and Remote Keyless Entry System (RKES). PKES allows users to unlock their vehicles automatically when they are in proximity, enhancing convenience and security without needing a physical key. In contrast, RKES relies on remote controls to unlock and start the vehicle from a distance, offering similar benefits. Both systems aim to improve user experience by providing keyless access and enhancing vehicle security.</p></p>
