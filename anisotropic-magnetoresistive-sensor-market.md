<p><p>Anisotropic Magnetoresistive Sensor market consists of three main types: 1 Axis Magnetic Sensor, 2 Axis Magnetic Sensor, and 3 Axis Magnetic Sensor. 1 Axis Sensor measures magnetic field in one direction, mainly used in applications where only one component of magnetic field needs to be detected. 2 Axis Sensor measures magnetic field in two orthogonal directions, suitable for applications requiring 2D magnetic field detection. 3 Axis Sensor measures magnetic field in all three directions, ideal for applications that require full 3D magnetic field sensing capabilities.</p></p>
