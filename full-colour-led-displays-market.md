<p><strong>The Full Colour LED Displays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SMD</li><li>COB</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The full-color LED display market is primarily categorized into SMD (Surface-Mounted Diode) and COB (Chip-on-Board) technologies. SMD displays utilize individual diodes mounted on a circuit board, offering high resolutions and flexibility for various applications, from advertising to event displays. COB technology, on the other hand, integrates multiple LED chips directly onto a substrate, enhancing brightness and color uniformity while reducing the overall size. Both types cater to different needs, balancing factors like cost, performance, and aesthetic appeal in diverse settings.</p></p>
