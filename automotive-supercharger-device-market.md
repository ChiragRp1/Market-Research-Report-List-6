<p><strong>The Automotive Supercharger Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Twin-screw Supercharger</li><li>Centrifugal Supercharges</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive supercharger device market primarily features two types: twin-screw superchargers and centrifugal superchargers. Twin-screw superchargers use intermeshing rotors to compress air, offering high efficiency and immediate power delivery, making them suitable for applications requiring rapid throttle response. On the other hand, centrifugal superchargers utilize a rotating impeller to increase air pressure, providing smooth power delivery at higher RPMs, which is ideal for performance-oriented vehicles. Both types enhance engine performance and efficiency, catering to diverse automotive needs.</p></p>
