<p><p>The medical imaging arms market comprises three main types: C-Arm, O-Arm, and G-Arm. C-Arms are versatile imaging devices used mainly in fluoroscopy and surgical procedures, providing real-time X-ray images. O-Arms are advanced imaging systems designed for 2D and 3D imaging, primarily utilized in spinal surgeries. G-Arms combine the features of C-Arms and O-Arms, offering enhanced imaging capabilities for a broader range of surgical applications. Each arm type plays a critical role in improving surgical precision and patient outcomes.</p></p>
