<p><p>Inhalational anaesthesia drugs market includes types such as Enflurane, Isoflurane, Halothane, Sevoflurane, and Atropine. Enflurane is a volatile liquid used for general anesthesia. Isoflurane is a halogenated ether used for inhalation anesthesia. Halothane is a nonflammable, halogenated general anesthetic. Sevoflurane is a sweet-smelling fluorinated methyl isopropyl ether. Atropine is used to prevent or treat bradycardia. Each of these drugs plays a vital role in ensuring safe and effective anesthesia administration in medical procedures.</p></p>
