<p><p>The video intercom system market comprises three main types: door stations, video intercom masters, and indoor units. Door stations are installed at entry points, enabling communication and video display of visitors. Video intercom masters serve as the central control units, allowing users to manage communication between door stations and indoor units. Indoor units are placed within homes or offices, providing users with a visual interface to interact with visitors. Together, these components enhance security and convenience in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
