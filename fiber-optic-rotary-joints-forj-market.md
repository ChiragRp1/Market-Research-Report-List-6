<p><p>Fiber Optic Rotary Joints (FORJ) are devices that facilitate the transfer of optical signals between stationary and rotating components, commonly used in applications like aerospace and industrial machinery. The market can be categorized into two main types: Single-Channel and Multi-Channel. Single-Channel FORJs transmit a single optical signal, often suited for basic applications. In contrast, Multi-Channel FORJs handle multiple signals simultaneously, allowing for enhanced data transfer capabilities, making them ideal for complex systems requiring high bandwidth and extensive communication.</p></p>
