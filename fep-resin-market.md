<p><p>The FEP (Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene) resin market is primarily divided into two main types: extrusion grade and injection grade. Extrusion grade FEP resin is utilized for producing films, sheets, and tubing, characterized by its excellent flow properties and high thermal stability. In contrast, injection grade FEP resin is designed for precision molding applications, offering improved mechanical strength and enhanced dimensional stability. Both grades cater to diverse industries, including automotive, electronics, and chemical processing, owing to their unique properties and performance capabilities.</p></p>
