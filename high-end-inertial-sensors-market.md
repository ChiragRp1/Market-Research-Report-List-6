GNSS receivers provide accurate positioning data through satellite signals. These sensors are critical in applications such as navigation, robotics, and autonomous systems, enhancing performance and precision in diverse industries like aerospace, automotive, and consumer electronics by enabling reliable motion tracking and positioning capabilities.</p></p>
