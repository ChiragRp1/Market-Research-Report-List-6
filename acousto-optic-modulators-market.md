<p><p>The Acousto-Optic Modulators (AOM) market is segmented into two main types: Fiber-Coupled Acousto-Optic Modulators and Free-Space Acousto-Optic Modulators. Fiber-Coupled AOMs are designed for integration with optical fibers, offering stability and compactness for telecommunications and sensing applications. In contrast, Free-Space AOMs operate in open air or vacuum environments, catering to applications requiring direct light modulation, such as laser systems and imaging technologies. Both types play critical roles in precision control of light in various industries.</p></p>
