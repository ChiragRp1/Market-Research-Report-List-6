<p><strong>The Construction Elastomers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic</li><li>Thermoset Plastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The construction elastomers market is primarily divided into two categories: thermoplastic and thermoset elastomers. Thermoplastic elastomers (TPEs) are flexible, can be reshaped upon heating, and offer excellent processability, making them suitable for various applications like seals and gaskets. In contrast, thermoset elastomers are cross-linked during curing, resulting in materials that are more rigid and heat-resistant, ideal for applications requiring durability, such as flooring and roofing materials. Both types are essential for enhancing performance in construction applications.</p></p>
