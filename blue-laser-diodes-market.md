<p><p>The blue laser diode market is categorized into single-mode and multi-mode types. Single-mode blue laser diodes emit coherent light, focusing on a narrow beam, making them ideal for applications requiring precision, such as telecommunications and high-density optical storage. In contrast, multi-mode blue laser diodes generate light across multiple modes, resulting in a broader beam, suitable for applications like projectors and lighting where higher power and broader coverage are essential. Both types cater to diverse technological demands and industries.</p></p>
