<p><p>Wooden kitchen benchtops can be divided into three main market types: solid wood, laminated wood, and glued wood. Solid wood benchtops are made from a single solid piece of wood, providing a natural and luxurious look. Laminated wood benchtops consist of a thin layer of wood bonded to a substrate, offering a more affordable option with a variety of designs. Glued wood benchtops are created by gluing multiple pieces of wood together, combining the strength of solid wood with the flexibility of laminate options.</p></p>
