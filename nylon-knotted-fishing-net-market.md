<p><p>Nylon knotted fishing nets can be categorized into two main market types based on the orientation of the knots: Depthway and Lengthway. Depthway nets have knots that run vertically from top to bottom, providing more strength and durability for capturing larger fish. On the other hand, Lengthway nets have knots that run horizontally across the length of the net, making them more suitable for catching smaller fish. Each market type caters to different fishing needs and preferences, offering versatility and efficiency for fishermen.</p></p>
