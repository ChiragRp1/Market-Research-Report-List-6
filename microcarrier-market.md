<p><ul><li>Cationic Microcarriers</li><li>Collagen-coated Microcarriers</li><li>Protein-coated Microcarriers</li><li>Untreated Microcarriers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microcarrier market comprises various types tailored for specific cell culture applications. Cationic microcarriers enhance cell attachment through positive charges, promoting higher yield in bioprocesses. Collagen-coated microcarriers mimic natural extracellular matrix environments, benefiting cell growth and differentiation. Protein-coated microcarriers offer a similar advantage by providing cellular adhesion factors. Untreated microcarriers provide a versatile base for diverse cell types, allowing researchers to modify them as needed. Together, these microcarrier types support advancements in biotechnology and regenerative medicine.</p></p>
