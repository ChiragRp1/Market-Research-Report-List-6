<p><p>Online tutoring markets are primarily categorized into structured tutoring and on-demand tutoring. Structured tutoring involves planned sessions with a fixed curriculum, often resembling traditional classroom settings, where learners progress through predefined materials with consistent schedules. In contrast, on-demand tutoring offers flexibility, allowing students to access help whenever needed, typically through instant messaging or video calls. This model caters to immediate learning needs, promoting a more responsive educational experience tailored to individual student challenges.</p></p>
