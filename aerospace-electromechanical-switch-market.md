<p><p>The aerospace electromechanical switch market encompasses various types, each serving specific functions in aviation applications. Tactile switches offer feedback when pressed, while rotary switches enable users to select different settings. Encoders convert rotational position into electrical signals, and toggle switches allow for quick on/off operations. Push switches are activated with a simple press, whereas detect switches sense and respond to external inputs. Micro, DIP, and other variants like power, slide, and joystick switches cater to diverse operational needs within aerospace systems.</p></p>
