<p><p>Balsa core materials are categorized into monolayer and multilayer market types, each serving distinct applications. In contrast, multilayer balsa cores are composed of multiple layers, providing enhanced strength, durability, and insulation. This layered structure caters to more demanding needs in aerospace, marine, and automotive industries, where performance and weight optimization are critical for competitive advantage.</p></p>
