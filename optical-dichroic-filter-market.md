<p><p>The optical dichroic filter market includes various types serving distinct applications. Bandpass filters transmit specific wavelength ranges while blocking others. UV-bandpass filters focus solely on ultraviolet light. Longpass filters allow wavelengths longer than a set point, whereas shortpass filters do the opposite, letting shorter wavelengths through. NIR cut filters block near-infrared light, protecting sensors from excessive heat. Notch filters eliminate a precise wavelength, preventing interference. Additionally, "Others" encompasses specialized filters tailored for unique applications across industries such as photography, telecommunications, and medical imaging.</p></p>
