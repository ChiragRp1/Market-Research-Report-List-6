<p><p>Photovoltaic convergence boxes are essential components in solar energy systems, serving as junction points for connecting solar panels to inverters and the grid. The market for these boxes can be categorized into two types: DC convergence boxes, which aggregate the direct current output from multiple solar panels before it is converted to alternating current; and AC convergence boxes, which manage the alternating current that has already been inverted. Both types enhance system efficiency and reliability, facilitating smoother installation and operation.</p></p>
