<p><p>The aloe extract market is primarily divided into two types: Aloe Gel Extracts and Aloe Whole Leaf Extracts. Aloe Gel Extracts are derived from the inner gel of the aloe vera leaf, valued for their soothing and hydrating properties, commonly used in cosmetics and skincare. In contrast, Aloe Whole Leaf Extracts utilize the entire leaf, incorporating both inner gel and outer skin, offering a broader range of nutrients and applications, including digestive health and nutritional supplements. Both types are popular in the wellness and personal care industries.</p></p>
