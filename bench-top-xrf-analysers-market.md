<p><strong>The Bench-top XRF Analysers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wavelength Dispersive (WDXRF)</li><li>Energy Dispersive (EDXRF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bench-top XRF (X-ray fluorescence) analyzers are categorized into two main types: Wavelength Dispersive XRF (WDXRF) and Energy Dispersive XRF (EDXRF). WDXRF offers high-resolution analysis by using monochromatic X-rays, making it ideal for precise elemental composition in complex matrices. In contrast, EDXRF provides faster and more versatile measurements over a broader range of elements, typically at lower costs. Both technologies are essential for applications in metallurgy, environmental monitoring, and quality control across various industries.</p></p>
