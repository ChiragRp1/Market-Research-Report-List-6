<p><strong>The Caramel Color Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Class I Caramel Color</li><li>Class II Caramel Color</li><li>Class III Caramel Color</li><li>Class IV Caramel Color</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The caramel color market is categorized into four classes based on their production methods and properties. Class I is unburned caramel, providing a light color and mild flavor, suitable for various applications. Class II, known as caustic sulfite caramel, has a medium color with a slightly stronger taste. Class III is produced using ammonia, offering a dark color and robust flavor, often used in beverages. Class IV, produced with ammonium compounds, provides the darkest hue and is typically used in food products requiring deep coloration.</p></p>
