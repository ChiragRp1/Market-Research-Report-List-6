<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Structural Steel Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spiral Weld Tube</li><li>Double Submerged Arc Weld (DSAW) Tube</li><li>Electric Resistance Weld (ERW) Tube</li><li>Seamless (SMLS) Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The structural steel tube market comprises various types based on manufacturing processes. Spiral Weld Tubes feature a spiral seam for strength and flexibility, ideal for large diameters. Double Submerged Arc Weld (DSAW) Tubes utilize submerged arc welding on both sides for robust joints and high-quality finishes. Electric Resistance Weld (ERW) Tubes are produced by high-frequency electrical resistance, offering cost-effectiveness and uniformity. Seamless (SMLS) Tubes are crafted without welds, ensuring superior strength and integrity, often used in high-pressure applications.</p></p>
