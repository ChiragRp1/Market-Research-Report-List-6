<p><p>The IoT smart pressure sensors market is comprised of three primary types: Piezoelectric, Diffused Silicon, and Sapphire sensors. Piezoelectric sensors convert pressure into electrical charges, offering high sensitivity and wide frequency response, ideal for dynamic measurements. Diffused Silicon sensors utilize silicon technology for accurate, stable pressure readings in various environments. Sapphire sensors, known for their durability and chemical resistance, deliver reliable performance in extreme conditions, making them suitable for harsh applications. Together, they cater to diverse industrial and consumer needs.</p></p>
