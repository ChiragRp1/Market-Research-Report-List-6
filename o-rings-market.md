<p><p>The O-ring market primarily consists of two types: nitrile rubber and FKM fluoroelastomers. Nitrile rubber, known for its excellent oil resistance and durability, is widely used in automotive and industrial applications. FKM fluoroelastomers, on the other hand, offer superior chemical resistance and high-temperature stability, making them ideal for aerospace and chemical processing industries. Both materials cater to diverse sectors, with nitrile rubber typically being more cost-effective, while FKM is preferred for harsh environments requiring enhanced performance.</p></p>
