<p><ul><li>Borrower-paid (BPMI)</li><li>Lender-paid (LPMI)</li><li>Single Premium</li><li>Split Premium</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lenders Mortgage Insurance comes in different market types - Borrower-paid (BPMI), Lender-paid (LPMI), Single Premium, and Split Premium. In BPMI, the borrower pays the insurance premium as part of their monthly mortgage payments. In LPMI, the lender covers the cost of insurance but may charge a higher interest rate to compensate. Single Premium involves paying the insurance premium upfront in a lump sum. Split Premium divides the insurance premium between an upfront payment and ongoing monthly payments. Each market type offers varying benefits and costs for borrowers.</p></p>
