<p><p>The vinyl siding market is divided into two main types: insulated siding and non-insulated siding. Insulated siding features a layer of foam backing, enhancing energy efficiency by reducing thermal transfer and improving overall comfort in homes. This type offers additional structural strength and noise reduction. In contrast, non-insulated siding lacks this foam layer, making it lighter and generally more affordable, but it provides less energy efficiency and insulation. Both options cater to different homeowner needs and preferences in terms of cost and performance.</p></p>
