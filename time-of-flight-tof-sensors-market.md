<p><strong>The Time-of-Flight (ToF) Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RF-Modulated Light Sources</li><li>Range Gated Imagers</li><li>Direct Time-Of-Flight Imagers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Time-of-Flight (ToF) sensors market comprises three key types: RF-Modulated Light Sources, Range Gated Imagers, and Direct Time-Of-Flight Imagers. RF-Modulated Light Sources use modulated laser or LED light to measure distance based on the phase shift of reflected light. Range Gated Imagers capture images at specific time intervals, allowing them to discern objects at varying distances. Direct Time-Of-Flight Imagers directly measure the time it takes for light to travel to an object and back, offering precise depth information for various applications.</p></p>
