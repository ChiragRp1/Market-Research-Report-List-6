<p><p>B-type Diagnostic Ultrasound Devices market includes three main types: Fanshaped Scanning, Linear Scanning, and Mixed Type. Fanshaped Scanning devices emit ultrasound waves in a fan-shaped pattern for a wider field of view. Linear Scanning devices use a linear transducer to produce high-resolution images of structures near the skin surface. Mixed Type devices combine both fanshaped and linear scanning capabilities for versatile imaging options. Each type caters to different clinical needs and preferences, offering a range of options for healthcare providers and patients.</p></p>
