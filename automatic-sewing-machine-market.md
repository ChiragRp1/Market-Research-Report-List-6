<p><p>The automatic sewing machine market is primarily divided into two types: single head and multi head sewing machines. Single head sewing machines are designed for individual projects, offering precision and versatility for small-scale production, often favored in custom garment manufacturing. In contrast, multi head sewing machines feature multiple needles, allowing for simultaneous stitching, significantly boosting output for high-volume applications like commercial embroidery. Both types cater to different production needs, enhancing efficiency in textile and apparel industries while accommodating varying design complexities.</p></p>
