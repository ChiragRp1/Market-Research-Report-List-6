<p><ul><li>Rotating Type</li><li>Non-Rotating Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydraulic liner hanger systems are crucial in the oil and gas industry for securely positioning liners in wells. The market features two primary types: rotating and non-rotating. Rotating type hangers allow for the rotation of the liner during cementing, facilitating better cement bonding and reducing potential for stuck pipes. In contrast, non-rotating types prevent rotation, offering stability and simplifying installation. Both systems cater to specific well conditions and operational requirements, influencing their deployment in various drilling scenarios.</p></p>
