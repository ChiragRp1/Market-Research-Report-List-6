<p><strong>The Powdered Pectin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Methoxyl (HM) Pectin</li><li>Low Methoxyl (LMC) Pectin</li><li>Amidated low Methoxyl (LMA) Pectin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The powdered pectin market is segmented into three main types: High Methoxyl (HM) Pectin, Low Methoxyl (LMC) Pectin, and Amidated Low Methoxyl (LMA) Pectin. HM Pectin requires high sugar concentrations for gelling and is commonly used in fruit preserves. LMC Pectin can gel with lower sugar levels and is ideal for sugar-free products. LMA Pectin, modified with amidation, offers flexibility in gelling conditions and caters to various food applications, including dairy and sauces, while maintaining texture and stability.</p></p>
