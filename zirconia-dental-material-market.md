<p><p>The Zirconia Dental Material Market includes two main types: Zirconia Dental Discs and Zirconia Dental Blocks. Zirconia Dental Discs are thin, circular pieces of zirconia used in milling machines to create dental restorations like crowns and bridges. Zirconia Dental Blocks are larger, solid blocks of zirconia that can be milled to create larger restorations like implant abutments and full arch restorations. Both types of zirconia materials offer high strength, durability, and aesthetics, making them popular choices in the dental industry for long-lasting and natural-looking restorations.</p></p>
