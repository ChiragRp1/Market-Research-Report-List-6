<p><ul><li>Accelerometers</li><li>Gyroscopes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive inertial sensor market primarily includes accelerometers and gyroscopes, vital for enhancing vehicle safety and performance. Accelerometers measure linear acceleration, providing data for functions like anti-lock braking systems and stability control. Gyroscopes, on the other hand, measure angular velocity, aiding in navigation and vehicle dynamics by detecting changes in orientation. Together, these sensors enable advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS), improve accident prevention mechanisms, and contribute to the development of autonomous driving technologies, thus driving market growth.</p></p>
