<p><p>The cannabis seeds market is categorized into three main types: Regular Seeds, Feminized Seeds, and Autoflowering Seeds. Regular Seeds produce both male and female plants, allowing for natural breeding but requiring careful management to prevent unwanted pollination. Feminized Seeds are specifically bred to produce only female plants, maximizing floral yield for consumers. Autoflowering Seeds transition from vegetative to flowering stage based on age rather than light cycles, offering quicker harvests and easier cultivation, appealing to novice and experienced growers alike.</p></p>
