Lithium-Ion (Li-Ion) batteries provide lightweight, compact solutions with excellent energy density and longevity. Lead-Acid batteries are cost-effective and widely used, though heavier and requiring more maintenance. Each type balances performance, cost, and application suitability in emergency lighting systems.</p></p>
