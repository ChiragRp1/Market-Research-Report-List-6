<p><p>Immunohistochemistry (IHC) testing market includes various methods for detecting specific antigens in tissue samples. The traditional direct method uses labeled antibodies to bind directly to antigens. The new direct method enhances sensitivity and specificity with improved reagents. The enzyme bridge method employs enzyme-labeled secondary antibodies for signal amplification, while the PAP (Peroxidase-Anti-Peroxidase) method utilizes a complex of antibodies and enzymes for a stronger signal. Each method offers unique advantages, driving diverse applications in diagnostics and research.</p></p>
