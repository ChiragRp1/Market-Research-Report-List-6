<p><strong>The Mobile Network Booster Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Analog Signal Booster</li><li>Digital Signal Booster</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mobile network booster market is primarily segmented into analog and digital signal boosters. Analog signal boosters enhance weak cellular signals by amplifying and retransmitting them without digital processing, making them simpler and often more affordable. In contrast, digital signal boosters convert incoming signals into digital format, allowing for advanced processing and better signal quality, particularly in densely populated areas. Both types aim to improve mobile connectivity, but their technologies and applications cater to different needs and environments.</p></p>
