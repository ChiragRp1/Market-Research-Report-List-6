<p><p>The Food and Beverage Coding and Marking Equipment market comprises various technologies tailored for labeling and marking products. CIJ (Continuous Inkjet) technology offers high-speed coding on varied surfaces. Laser technology uses focused light for permanent markings, ideal for durable applications. TTO (Thermal Transfer Overprinting) technology provides high-quality prints on flexible materials. PALM (Print and Apply Labeling Machine) automates label application. PIJ (Piezo Inkjet) and TIJ (Thermal Inkjet) technologies are employed for precise prints. VIJ (Valveless Inkjet) technology enhances efficiency, while primary packaging code focuses on essential product information.</p></p>
