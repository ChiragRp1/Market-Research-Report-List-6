<p><strong>The Suspension Parts of Excavators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rigid Connected Type</li><li>Semi-Floating</li><li>Full Floating</li><li>Independent Mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The suspension parts of excavators play a crucial role in ensuring stability and performance. There are four main types: </p><p>1. **Rigid Connected Type**: Features a solid framework that connects the suspension across the entire structure, offering simple design but limited flexibility. </p><p>2. **Semi-Floating**: Combines features of both rigid and floating, providing moderate flexibility while maintaining some structure stability. </p><p>3. **Full Floating**: Allows maximum movement, enhancing adaptability to varying terrains, ensuring better comfort and handling.</p><p>4. **Independent Mounted**: Each wheel or track operates independently, optimizing traction and stability across uneven surfaces.</p></p>
