<p><p>Hydrogen peroxide sterilization equipment can be categorized into two main types: Vaporized Hydrogen Peroxide Sterilization Equipment and Fogging Hydrogen Peroxide Sterilization Equipment. Vaporized equipment uses hydrogen peroxide in a gaseous state to penetrate and sterilize various surfaces and equipment. Fogging equipment, on the other hand, disperses a fine mist of hydrogen peroxide solution to sterilize large areas quickly and effectively. Both types of equipment are widely used in healthcare settings, laboratories, and other industries requiring stringent sterilization processes.</p></p>
