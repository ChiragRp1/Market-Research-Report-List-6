<p><p>The non-woven adhesive market primarily includes SBC-based adhesives and APAO-based adhesives. SBC-based adhesives, derived from styrene block copolymers, offer flexibility and good adhesion, making them suitable for applications in hygiene products and personal care. In contrast, APAO-based adhesives, or amorphous polyalphaoefins, provide excellent temperature resistance and bonding strength, ideal for packaging and automotive industries. Both types serve distinct applications, catering to the diverse needs of the non-woven fabric sector, where efficiency and performance are critical.</p></p>
