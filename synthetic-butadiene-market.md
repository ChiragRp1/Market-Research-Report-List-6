<p><p>The synthetic butadiene market encompasses various production processes, including oxidative dehydrogenation and extractive distillation. Oxidative dehydrogenation involves the conversion of hydrocarbons into butadiene using oxidative catalysts, enhancing efficiency and reducing byproducts. Extractive distillation, on the other hand, separates butadiene from other C4 hydrocarbons using solvents to improve purity. Both methods play a crucial role in meeting the rising demand for butadiene in the rubber and plastics industries, impacting pricing and production strategies in the market.</p></p>
