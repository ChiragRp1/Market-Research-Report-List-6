<p><p>The medical pressure sensors market includes MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) based medical pressure sensors and pressure transducers. MEMS sensors utilize miniaturized mechanical components to measure pressure with high precision and sensitivity, making them ideal for wearable devices and diagnostics. Pressure transducers convert pressure into an electrical signal, enabling accurate monitoring in various medical applications, such as blood pressure measurement and respiratory monitoring. Both types enhance healthcare technologies by providing reliable data for effective patient management.</p></p>
