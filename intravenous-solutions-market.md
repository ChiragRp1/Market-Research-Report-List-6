<p><p>The intravenous solutions market is categorized into three main types: crystalloids, colloids, and others. Crystalloids are aqueous solutions of salts that can maintain fluid balance and electrolyte levels, often used for hydration. Colloids contain larger molecules that help maintain osmotic pressure, making them suitable for volume expansion in critical care. Other solutions may include specialized formulations or additives tailored for specific medical conditions or patient needs, contributing to overall patient management and therapeutic outcomes.</p></p>
