<p><p>AS-i market includes AS-i Master/Gateway, AS-i Power Supply, AS-i Cable, and AS-i Slave components. AS-i Master/Gateway acts as a communication interface between the AS-i network and higher-level control systems. AS-i Power Supply provides power to the network. AS-i Cable physically connects the devices and transfers data. AS-i Slave devices are the end-point components in the network that gather and transmit data. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring the efficient operation of the AS-i system.</p></p>
