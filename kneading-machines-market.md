<p><p>Kneading machines are categorized into different types based on their operating mechanisms. Spiral kneaders involve a spiral hook that moves in the mixing bowl to knead the dough evenly. Planetary kneaders have dual motions of both revolving the bowl and rotating the mixing attachment to ensure thorough mixing. Other types of kneading machines include traditional mixers and horizontal mixers. Each type offers unique features and benefits, catering to different baking and food processing needs in the market.</p></p>
