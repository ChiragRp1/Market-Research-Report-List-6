<p><strong>The Synthetic Brake Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DOT 3</li><li>DOT 4</li><li>DOT 5</li><li>DOT 5.1</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The synthetic brake fluid market encompasses various types, including DOT 3, DOT 4, DOT 5, and DOT 5.1 fluids. DOT 3 is glycol-based, suitable for standard vehicles, while DOT 4 offers improved performance with higher boiling points. DOT 5 is silicone-based, non-hygroscopic, and ideal for classic vehicles, though incompatible with glycol fluids. DOT 5.1, similar in performance to DOT 4, is also glycol-based but offers superior thermal stability and is compatible with other glycol fluids, making it suitable for high-performance applications.</p></p>
