<p><p>Refrigerated air dryers are essential for removing moisture from compressed air systems. In the market, they are categorized into two main types: cycling and non-cycling. Cycling dryers adjust their cooling capacity based on demand, effectively saving energy and reducing operational costs. In contrast, non-cycling dryers maintain a constant level of cooling, which ensures continuous performance but can lead to higher energy consumption. Both types cater to different operational needs and efficiency preferences in various industries.</p></p>
