<p><p>The digital protective relay market encompasses various types designed for specific protection functions in electrical systems. Overcurrent protection relays safeguard against excessive current conditions, while arc protection relays detect and mitigate dangerous arcing faults. Motor protection relays monitor motor health and prevent damage from overloads. Transformer protection relays ensure the safety of transformers from faults. Capacitor bank protection relays manage the health of capacitor systems, and other relays cover additional applications like earth faults and differential protection, enhancing overall system reliability.</p></p>
