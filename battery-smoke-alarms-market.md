<p><p>Battery smoke alarms come in three main types: Photoelectric Type, Ionization Type, and Others. Photoelectric Type alarms use a light-sensitive sensor to detect slow smoldering fires, while Ionization Type alarms use a radioactive source to detect fast flaming fires. The Others category typically includes combination alarms that incorporate both photoelectric and ionization technology, as well as specialized alarms for specific uses such as carbon monoxide detection or hearing-impaired individuals. Each type offers different advantages and is suitable for different types of fires.</p></p>
