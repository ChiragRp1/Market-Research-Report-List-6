<p><p>Customer Experience Management (CEM) comes in two main market types: Cloud-Based and On-Premise. Cloud-Based CEM solutions are hosted on the vendor's servers and accessed through the internet, providing scalability and flexibility for businesses. On-Premise CEM solutions are installed and managed on the company's own servers, offering greater control and security. Both market types enable businesses to gather and analyze customer feedback, improve processes, and enhance customer satisfaction. The choice between the two depends on factors such as budget, IT infrastructure, and desired level of customization.</p></p>
