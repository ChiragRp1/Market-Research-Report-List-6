<p><p>The UAV autopilot market includes three primary types: </p><p>1. **Full Automatic UAV Autopilot**: These systems autonomously control all flight operations, minimizing human intervention and enhancing efficiency.</p><p>2. **Computer Assisted Flight UAV Autopilot**: This type offers a blend of automated and manual controls, assisting pilots with navigation and stability while allowing human oversight.</p><p>3. **Manual Flight UAV Autopilot**: In this configuration, operators maintain full control over the UAV, using autopilot primarily for basic stabilization and support functions during flight.</p></p>
