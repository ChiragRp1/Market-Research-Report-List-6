<p><p>LiDAR technology encompasses various market types based on their operational platforms. Aerial LiDAR utilizes aircraft to scan large areas, providing high-resolution data for topographic mapping. Ground-based LiDAR involves stationary setups, ideal for detailed scans of structures or landscapes. Mobile LiDAR employs moving vehicles equipped with LiDAR sensors, capturing data along roadways efficiently. UAV LiDAR integrates unmanned aerial vehicles for flexible, low-altitude surveying, making it suitable for challenging terrains. Each type serves distinct applications in mapping, engineering, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
