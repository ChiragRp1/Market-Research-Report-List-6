<p><p>The low horsepower AC motors market is primarily divided into synchronous and asynchronous motors. Synchronous motors operate at a constant speed in sync with the supply frequency, offering precise control and efficiency, often used in applications where speed stability is crucial. In contrast, asynchronous motors, also known as induction motors, operate at varying speeds based on load conditions, making them versatile and widely used in general applications due to their simplicity and robustness. Each type serves distinct industrial needs and preferences.</p></p>
