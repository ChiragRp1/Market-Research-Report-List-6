<p><p>The cranberry powder market primarily consists of two types: freeze-dried powder and juice-pressed powder. Freeze-dried powder retains nutrients and flavor by removing moisture through low-temperature dehydration, resulting in a concentrated and potent product that is ideal for smoothies, supplements, and baking. In contrast, juice-pressed powder is derived from pressed cranberries, preserving the juice's freshness and taste, making it suitable for beverages and flavored products. Both types cater to health-conscious consumers seeking antioxidant-rich, natural ingredients.</p></p>
