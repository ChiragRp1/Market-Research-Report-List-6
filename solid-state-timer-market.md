<p><strong>The Solid State Timer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Resistor Adjustable</li><li>External Resistor Fixed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solid state timer market can be divided into two main types: external resistor adjustable and external resistor fixed timers. External resistor adjustable timers allow users to modify timing intervals by changing external resistors, providing flexibility for various applications. In contrast, external resistor fixed timers have preset timing durations predetermined by internal circuitry, offering simplicity and ease of use. Both types cater to different user needs in automation, control systems, and electronic device applications, highlighting their versatility in timing solutions.</p></p>
