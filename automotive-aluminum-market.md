<p><ul><li>Cast Aluminum</li><li>Rolled Aluminum</li><li>Extruded Aluminum</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive aluminum market comprises three main types: cast aluminum, rolled aluminum, and extruded aluminum. Cast aluminum is used for complex shapes and parts, typically in engine components and wheels. Rolled aluminum is processed into flat sheets or foils, often utilized for vehicle body panels and structures due to its lightweight and corrosion resistance. Extruded aluminum is formed into specific profiles, making it ideal for structural components, frame elements, and heat exchangers, enhancing the overall efficiency and performance of vehicles.</p></p>
