<p><p>The anti-snoring ventilator market is primarily divided into two types: single-level ventilators and bi-level ventilators. Single-level ventilators deliver a consistent airflow pressure throughout the breathing cycle, effectively reducing snoring by maintaining open airways during sleep. In contrast, bi-level ventilators provide varying pressures for inhalation and exhalation, allowing for more comfortable breathing patterns. Both types aim to enhance sleep quality and reduce the effects of sleep apnea and snoring, catering to different needs and preferences among users.</p></p>
