<p><p>Flash glucose monitoring encompasses two main market types: implantable monitoring systems and wearable monitoring systems. Implantable systems are devices placed under the skin that continuously measure glucose levels, providing real-time data without the need for external sensors. In contrast, wearable monitoring systems are external devices that attach to the skin and use sensors to detect glucose levels. Both types aim to enhance diabetes management, offering users greater convenience, improved accuracy, and timely insights into their glucose fluctuations.</p></p>
