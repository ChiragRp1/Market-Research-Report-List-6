<p><p>Optical data communication market types include Wavelength Division Multiplexing (WDM), which allows multiple signals to be transmitted simultaneously on different wavelengths; Synchronous Optical Networking (SONET) and Synchronous Digital Hierarchy (SDH), which provide standardized protocols for transmitting data over optical networks; Fiber Channel, which is a high-speed network technology primarily used in storage area networks. Other markets in optical data communication include emerging technologies like free-space optics and passive optical networks, offering alternative solutions for high-speed data transmission.</p></p>
