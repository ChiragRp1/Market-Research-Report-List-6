<p><strong>The Distributed Fibre Optic Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rayleigh Scattering Based Distributed Sensor</li><li>Brillouin Scattering Based Sensor</li><li>Raman Scattering Based Sensor</li><li>Interferometric Distributed Optical-Fibre Sensor</li><li>Distributed Fibre Bragg Grating Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Distributed Fibre Optic Sensors market features several key types. Rayleigh Scattering Based Sensors utilize backscatter from random microscopic variations in the fibre to measure strain and temperature. Brillouin Scattering Based Sensors leverage changes in frequency due to acoustic waves to assess strain and temperature changes over long distances. Raman Scattering Based Sensors focus on temperature variations by analyzing inelastic scattering. Interferometric Sensors measure phase changes caused by environmental factors, while Fibre Bragg Grating Sensors use periodic variations in refractive index for precise measurements.</p></p>
