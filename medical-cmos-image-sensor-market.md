<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Medical CMOS Image Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FSI</li><li>BSI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical CMOS Image Sensor market is categorized into two main types: Front-Side Illumination (FSI) and Back-Side Illumination (BSI). FSI sensors capture images using light that enters from the front, making them suitable for cost-effective applications. In contrast, BSI sensors feature a structure that allows light to reach the photodiodes from the back, enhancing sensitivity and image quality, particularly in low-light conditions. The choice between FSI and BSI depends on specific application requirements in medical imaging technologies.</p></p>
