Fibrin adhesives are derived from natural clotting proteins used to promote wound healing and tissue repair. Mussel adhesive proteins, inspired by marine organisms, offer strong bonding properties in wet conditions, suitable for surgical applications. Other bioadhesives encompass a range of synthetic and natural materials that provide versatile adhesion for diverse medical uses, including drug delivery and tissue engineering.</p></p>
