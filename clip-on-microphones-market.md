<p><p>The clip-on microphones market consists of various types suited for different recording needs. Omnidirectional mics capture sound equally from all directions, making them versatile for interviews and presentations. Cardioid mics are sensitive primarily to sound from the front, minimizing background noise, ideal for solo recordings. Supercardioid mics offer a narrower pickup pattern, perfect for live performances and broadcast, focusing on the sound source while reducing ambient noise. Additional types may include lavalier and directional mics, catering to specific applications.</p></p>
