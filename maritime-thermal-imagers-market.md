<p><p>The Maritime Thermal Imagers market is categorized into two main types: uncooled and cooled thermal imagers. Uncooled thermal imagers operate at ambient temperatures, utilizing materials that change resistance when exposed to infrared radiation, making them compact and cost-effective. Cooled thermal imagers, on the other hand, employ cryogenically cooled sensors, enhancing sensitivity and performance, particularly in long-range detection. Both types are crucial for maritime surveillance, navigation, and safety, each offering distinct advantages based on operational requirements.</p></p>
