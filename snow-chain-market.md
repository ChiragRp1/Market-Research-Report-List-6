<p><strong>The Snow Chain Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nonmetal Snow Chain</li><li>Metal Snow Chain</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The snow chain market is primarily divided into two types: nonmetal and metal snow chains. Nonmetal snow chains, typically made from materials like rubber or polymer, offer lightweight options that provide good traction without damaging road surfaces. They are particularly beneficial for lighter vehicles and moderate snow conditions. In contrast, metal snow chains, constructed from steel links, deliver superior grip and durability, making them ideal for heavy vehicles and extreme winter conditions. Each type serves distinct user needs and driving environments.</p></p>
