Hybridization involves the binding of complementary DNA strands, enabling the identification of genetic variations or pathogens. DNA sequencing decodes the precise order of nucleotides in DNA, allowing for comprehensive genomic studies. These methods collectively enhance disease diagnosis, personalized medicine, and genomics research, driving growth in the market.</p></p>
