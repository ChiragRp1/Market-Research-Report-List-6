<p><p>The differential-pressure level gauge market includes two primary types: mechanical and electronic level gauges. Mechanical level gauges utilize a float and a differential pressure sensor to measure liquid levels, providing reliable and straightforward operation, often in simpler applications. In contrast, electronic level gauges employ advanced sensors and digital displays, offering higher accuracy and features like data logging and remote monitoring. Both types serve critical roles in various industries, ensuring precise level measurement in tanks and vessels, albeit with different operational mechanisms and applications.</p></p>
