<p><p>Polyamide wax is available in various forms in the market, such as granules, powder, and micro-powder. Granules are larger particles that are commonly used in applications where a slower melting point or controlled release is required. Powder forms of polyamide wax are finely ground particles, ideal for blending into formulations to improve flow and texture. Micro-powder, on the other hand, is even finer than powder form, offering enhanced dispersibility and compatibility with other ingredients. Each form of polyamide wax caters to specific application requirements in various industries.</p></p>
