<p><p>Actuator Sensor Interface Systems (AS-I) market includes various types of products such as AS-I Gateway/Master, AS-I Cable, AS-I Power Supply, AS-I Slave, Repeater, and Extension Plug. AS-I Gateway/Master facilitates communication between higher-level control systems and AS-I network. AS-I Cable provides the connection between devices in the AS-I network. AS-I Power Supply ensures proper power distribution within the network. AS-I Slave act as the interface between sensors/actuators and the AS-I network. Repeaters extend the range of the AS-I network, while Extension Plugs allow for easy expansion of the network.</p></p>
