They come in various channel configurations, including 1-channel, 2-channel, 3-channel, 7-channel, and 12-channel options. The channel count indicates the number of electrodes used to record heart activity; higher numbers provide more comprehensive data, enhancing diagnostic accuracy. While 1-channel monitors are simpler and cost-effective, multi-channel devices offer detailed insights into arrhythmias and other cardiac conditions, catering to diverse clinical needs.</p></p>
