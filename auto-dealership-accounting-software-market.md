<p><p>Auto dealership accounting software is available in two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based solutions are hosted on remote servers, allowing dealerships to access their financial data from anywhere via the internet, promoting flexibility and ease of updates. On-premises software, conversely, is installed locally on dealership servers, providing greater control over data security and customization but requiring more maintenance and upfront costs. Both types cater to the unique needs and preferences of auto dealerships in managing their finances.</p></p>
