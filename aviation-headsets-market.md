<p><strong>The Aviation Headsets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PNR headsets</li><li>ANR headsets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are two main types of aviation headsets in the market, Passive Noise Reduction (PNR) headsets and Active Noise Reduction (ANR) headsets. PNR headsets block out external noise through padding and insulation, providing a basic level of noise reduction. ANR headsets use technology to actively cancel out external noise, offering a higher level of noise reduction and improved clarity of communication. Both types of headsets are popular among pilots and aviation enthusiasts for their ability to enhance communication and reduce hearing damage in noisy environments.</p></p>
