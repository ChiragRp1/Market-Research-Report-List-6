Aluminum is lightweight and often more affordable, providing versatility and a modern aesthetic but may lack the longevity of stainless steel. Brass offers a classic, elegant appearance with natural antimicrobial properties, though it can require more maintenance to preserve its finish. Each material caters to different consumer preferences and functional needs in kitchen design.</p></p>
