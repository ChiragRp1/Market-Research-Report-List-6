<p><p>Flaxseed oil is extracted using several methods, each impacting its nutritional profile and market appeal. The squeezing method produces high-quality oil by mechanically pressing seeds, retaining nutrients. Hot pressing enhances yield but may reduce beneficial properties due to heat. Leaching method utilizes solvents for extraction, yielding more oil but potentially compromising purity and nutrition. These methods cater to different consumer preferences in the flaxseed oil market, with demand for premium, nutrient-rich oils driving growth in the squeezing method segment.</p></p>
